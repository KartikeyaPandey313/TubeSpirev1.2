Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2025

Our Core Principle: Your Privacy First

TubeSpire is designed to be a private and anonymous service. We believe you should be able to use our tool without worrying about your data being tracked or stored.

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what we do not do:

  • No User Accounts: We do not require you to sign up or create an account.
  • No IP Address Logging: We do not store your IP address in connection with your usage.
  • No Download History: The URLs you paste are processed in real-time and are never saved to a database.

Information We Do Collect

To maintain the health and security of our service, we collect a minimal amount of non-personally identifiable information:

  • Aggregated Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous traffic data, such as which pages are visited most often and the general country of origin of our visitors. This helps us understand how our service is used and where we can improve. This data is aggregated and cannot be tied to an individual user.
  • Error Logs: If our service encounters an error during a download request, our server may log the type of error and the video URL that caused it. This is purely for debugging purposes to help us fix bugs and is automatically deleted on a regular basis.

Cookies

We use a single, functional cookie (downloadComplete) to improve your user experience by hiding the loading animation after your file has been sent. This cookie contains no personal information and automatically expires.
